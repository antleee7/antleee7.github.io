I am interested in questions motivated by number theory, especially in the intersection of homogeneous dynamics and Lie theory. Much of my recent work is inspired by Selberg's Prime Geodesic Theorem and related counting problems for rank 1 Riemannian symmetric spaces.

Concretely, I've worked on questions involving arithmetic lattices of $\operatorname{PSL}_2(\mathbb{C})$, and I am currently interested in extending these ideas to arithmetic lattices of $\operatorname{SO}_0(n,1)$ for $n > 3$. More broadly, I am interested in understanding such counting problems for arithmetic subgroups of linear groups in general, including through volume formulas due to G. Prasad.
